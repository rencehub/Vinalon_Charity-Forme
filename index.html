Join our organization!

We are a conservation organization that works to protect and restore wildlife habitats. Students can get involved by participating in conservation projects or advocating for wildlife protection. Here are three reasons why you should support our federation:

  1. You can help preserve and restore natural habitats, protect endangered species, and promote biodiversity.
  2. Provide webinars, workshops, educational resources, and even career opportunities.
  3. Participate in advocacy efforts, sign petitions, and even write policies that support wildlife conservation.
